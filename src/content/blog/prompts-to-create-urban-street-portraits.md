Urban street style portraits capture that raw, real-city energy you see in fashion editorials and photo feeds. The right mix of lighting, outfit, and city backdrop can make a photo look like it was taken by a professional photographer. 📸 These prompts are made to help you create that exact look using Gemini AI, all from text descriptions, no reference photo needed.

To get started, open Gemini at gemini.google.com and look for the image generation section (you can also use Google ImageFX). Paste any of the prompts below into the text box and hit generate. You can swap out the outfit color, city vibe, or time of day to make each result fit your vision. 🎯

### 1. Create a Solo Woman Street Style Portrait

*“A photorealistic portrait of a young woman standing on a busy city sidewalk during golden hour. She is wearing an oversized camel blazer, high-waisted wide-leg jeans, and chunky white sneakers. Her hair is loose and slightly windblown. The warm late-afternoon sun hits her from the side, casting a soft glow on her skin. Behind her are blurred city buildings, parked cars, and pedestrians. Shot on a Sony A7R IV with an 85mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, editorial fashion photography style, 4K resolution.”*

### 2. Generate a Solo Man Street Style Portrait

*“A photorealistic full-body portrait of a young man standing on a neon-lit city street at night. He is wearing a black bomber jacket, slim-fit dark cargo pants, and white leather sneakers. He has a relaxed, confident posture with hands in pockets, looking directly at the camera. Neon signs from surrounding shops reflect on the wet pavement. Cinematic blue and purple color tones, shot on a Canon EOS R5 with a 50mm f/1.2 lens, street photography style, high detail, 4K resolution.”*

### 3. Create a Stylish Couple Portrait in an Urban Graffiti Alleyway

*“A photorealistic portrait of a stylish couple standing in a graffiti-covered urban alleyway. The woman wears a fitted leather jacket, plaid mini skirt, and ankle boots. The man wears a gray hoodie, straight-fit dark jeans, and clean white sneakers. They are laughing and looking at each other naturally. Soft overcast daylight with even shadows, shot on a Nikon Z6 II with a 35mm f/1.8 lens, candid street fashion style, warm and natural color grading, 4K resolution.”*

Great choice for a casual, natural-looking couple shoot. 💑
