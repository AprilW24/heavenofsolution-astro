Here are the steps to uninstall Google Play service updates for android users.

1. First of all, open **Settings**.

2. Then, click **Applications and Permissions**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Uninstall-Google-Play-Service-Updates-on-Android1.jpeg)

3. After that, select **App manager**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Uninstall-Google-Play-Service-Updates-on-Android2.jpeg)

4. Next, scroll to search **Google Play services** and click it.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Uninstall-Google-Play-Service-Updates-on-Android3.jpeg)

5. Last, click **Uninstall updates** and wait for the Google Play services will be deleted from your phone device.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Uninstall-Google-Play-Service-Updates-on-Android4.jpeg)
