Creating beauty product mockups with AI is one of the most practical moves right now, whether you’re a brand owner, a content creator, or someone who needs clean visuals without booking a photo studio. 🎨 Gemini AI can generate product images straight from a text prompt, meaning the details you write directly shape what comes out. The more specific the prompt, the more polished and on-brand the result gets.

To try these out, open gemini.google.com, look for the image generation option, paste in any prompt from below, and generate. Since these are creation prompts and not editing prompts, you don’t need to upload any reference image at all. 🖼️ You can swap out colors, props, or lighting details in any of these prompts to match your brand’s feel. Think of each one as a starting point you can build on.

### 1. Minimalist Skincare Flat Lay on White Marble Surface

*“A flat lay of a premium glass skincare serum bottle placed on a white marble surface, surrounded by fresh eucalyptus leaves and scattered dried chamomile flowers, soft diffused natural daylight coming from the left side, subtle water droplet details on the bottle surface, top-down overhead shot, editorial beauty photography style, high resolution, neutral tones with muted green accents, clean white background.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Minimalist-Skincare-Flat.webp)

### 2. Moody Luxury Perfume Bottle on Dark Obsidian Stone

*“A luxury crystal perfume bottle with deep amber liquid inside, placed on a dark obsidian stone slab, surrounded by dried black rose petals and golden chain accessories, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting with a single spotlight from above casting long soft shadows, dark moody background in deep charcoal gray, glossy reflections on the bottle surface, cinematic beauty campaign style, high-end fragrance editorial photography.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Moody-Luxury-Perfume.webp)

### 3. All-White Monochrome Skincare Product Still Life

*“A monochrome product still life featuring a matte white ceramic face moisturizer jar and a matching cleanser tube, arranged on an off-white linen cloth with soft natural folds and visible texture, all-white color palette with barely-there shadows, side lighting from a large diffused window, soft blurred background, luxury skincare brand photography, minimal and refined composition, high resolution.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_All-White-Monochrome-Skincare.webp)

Clean, medical-grade aesthetic. Works great for premium skincare brands going for a clinical or dermatologist-approved vibe. 🤍

### 4. Floating Glass Serum Bottle on Soft Blush Pink Background

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Floating-Glass-Serum.webp)

Perfect for brands going for a soft, dreamy, or *K-beauty* inspired look. 🌸

### 5. Botanical Flat Lay with Tropical Leaves and White Flowers

### 7. Dewy Facial Mist Bottle Captured Mid-Spray in Action

*“A clear glass facial mist spray bottle mid-spray against a clean white background, fine water mist particles bursting outward in all directions, frozen water droplets suspended in the air with visible light refraction, bright and fresh studio lighting with cool blue undertones, product label clearly facing forward, energetic and fresh skincare campaign aesthetic, high-speed photography style, sharp product detail with soft bokeh mist in the background.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Dewy-Facial-Mist-1024x595.webp)

These prompts are ready to paste straight into Gemini, just swap out the product type, colors, or props to match your brand. 🚀 The key is keeping your prompt detailed by thinking about lighting, surface, angles, and mood all at once. Once you get a result you like, you can keep fine-tuning it by adding follow-up instructions in the same chat.
