## What Perplexity Pro Brings to Your Android Experience

*Pro* isn’t just a badge-it changes how you use Perplexity. With *Pro*, you get to pick between advanced AI models like GPT-4 Omni, Claude 3 Sonnet, Sonar Large 32k, Grok 2, and Gemini 2.0 Flash. Each one has its own strengths, so you can match the model to your needs. You also get hundreds of *Pro* searches per day, perfect for research, work, or just satisfying your curiosity.

One of my favorite things? File uploads. With *Pro*, you can upload PDFs, images, and CSVs, then get detailed answers or summaries. This is super useful for students, professionals, or anyone who deals with a lot of documents. Plus, you get $5 in API credits each month, so you can plug Perplexity’s AI into your own projects if you’re into that sort of thing.

## Steps to activate Pro features on the Perplexity app

- Download or update the Perplexity app from the Google Play Store.

- Open the app and sign in with your Google or preferred account.

- Tap your profile icon or menu in the top corner.

- Find and tap on the *Pro* or *Upgrade* option.

- Review the *Pro* subscription details and pricing.

- Follow the prompts to subscribe using Google Play billing.

- Once subscribed, restart the app to refresh your features.

- Head back to the main screen and look for new *Pro* options-like model selection or file upload.

- Tap the model selector to switch between GPT-4, Claude, and others.

- Use the upload button to add PDFs, images, or CSVs for analysis.

## Using Perplexity Assistant and Getting the Most Out of Pro

The Perplexity Assistant is a game-changer for Android users. It lets you ask questions, control your device, and even make reservations or play music-all through gestures or voice. To get started, make sure you’ve updated to the latest version of the app. You’ll see an onboarding banner; tap it and follow the steps to set the Assistant as your default.

Once set up, you can activate the Assistant by swiping from the bottom-left corner, holding the power button, or holding the home button (depending on your device). With *Pro* active, you’ll notice even faster, more detailed responses, and you can handle more complex tasks. Try uploading a PDF or switching models to see the difference.

If you’re someone who juggles lots of info or just wants a smoother, more powerful AI experience, *Pro* is worth checking out. The combo of advanced models, file uploads, and the Assistant makes everyday tasks a lot easier-and a bit more fun.
