Gemini AI, powered by Google’s *Imagen 3* model, has gotten really good at generating realistic-looking photos from just a text description. You don’t need a camera, a photographer, or even a real beach to get gorgeous results. I’ve been testing a bunch of beach portrait prompts lately, and the results are seriously impressive. 🌊

To use these, head to gemini.google.com, start a new chat, and paste any prompt directly into the text box. Make sure you’re running *Gemini 2.0 Flash* or *Gemini Advanced* for the best image output quality. No reference photo is needed for any of these. Each prompt is written to generate a full beach portrait from scratch, so all you do is type and hit send. 📸

### 1. Solo Woman Beach Portrait with Golden Hour Lighting

*“A photorealistic portrait of a woman standing at the shoreline during golden hour, soft warm sunlight hitting her face from the side, a gentle ocean breeze blowing her long hair naturally. She is wearing a flowy white sundress with thin straps, barefoot on wet sand, looking softly toward the horizon with a relaxed smile. The sky behind her is painted in shades of amber, peach, and light pink. Shallow depth of field with the waves slightly blurred in the background. Shot with an 85mm lens, soft bokeh, editorial lifestyle photography style, high resolution, ultra-realistic skin texture.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Solo-Woman-Beach-657x1024.webp)

Works great for warm, dreamy solo portraits with a natural feminine feel.

The background shows a clear blue ocean horizon with soft white waves rolling in behind him. Shot from a slightly low angle to show more of the sky. Natural skin texture, cinematic color grading with teal and orange tones, 85mm portrait lens style, high resolution, ultra-realistic.”*

The sky behind them is a dramatic gradient of deep orange, red, and purple near the horizon. Shot with a 50mm lens, soft natural light, warm film-like color grading, cinematic photography style, high resolution.”*

These prompts give Gemini AI exactly the detail it needs to generate a realistic beach portrait straight from text. Mix and match the lighting, outfit, and setting details from different prompts to put together your own custom version. The more specific you get, the better your results will look. 🏖️
