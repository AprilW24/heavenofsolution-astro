Creating frames in Canva is a great way to add a stylish touch to your designs. Whether you’re using the desktop version or the mobile app, Canva offers various tools to help you make beautiful frames.

When it comes to designing, frames can really make your images pop. They add a professional look and can match any theme or style you’re aiming for. Whether you’re creating social media posts, presentations, or even just decorating a photo, frames are a simple yet effective way to enhance your visuals. Here’s how you can do it:

## Using Pre-Made Frames in Canva

If you want to use pre-made frames, Canva has a wide selection available. Here’s how to add them to your design:

1. Open Canva and create a new design.

2. Click on the “Elements” tab in the sidebar.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/6-Steps-to-Make-a-Frame-in-Canva-App.webp)

3. Type “frame” in the search bar and select a frame you like.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/6-Steps-to-Make-a-Frame-in-Canva-App1.webp)

4. Click on the frame to add it to your design.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2025/02/6-Steps-to-Make-a-Frame-in-Canva-App2.webp)

5. Insert an image into the frame by dragging it over the frame or using the upload option.

6. Adjust the image size and position within the frame.

## Creating Custom Frames in Canva

For a more personalized look, you can create custom frames using Canva’s tools or external apps like Frame Maker.

1. Open Canva and start a new design.

2. Go to the “Elements” tab and choose a shape or graphic that you want to use as a frame.

3. Edit the shape to fit your design needs.

4. Save the shape as a PDF or PNG file.

5. Import the file back into Canva to use it as a custom frame.

## Using Frame Maker App

If you prefer a more streamlined process, you can use the Frame Maker app:

1. Open Frame Maker and select your design.

2. Click “Convert Design to Frame.”

3. Choose the file format (e.g., PNG) and export the frame.

4. Import the frame into Canva and add your image.

## Conclusion

Creating frames in Canva is straightforward and allows you to customize your designs with ease. Whether you use pre-made frames or create your own, Canva provides the tools you need to make your designs stand out.

In addition to frames, Canva offers a wide range of design elements that can further enhance your creations. From backgrounds to text styles, you can experiment with different combinations to find the perfect look for your project. Plus, Canva’s user-friendly interface makes it easy for beginners and pros alike to create stunning visuals.

## FAQs

### How do I add an image to a frame in Canva?

To add an image to a frame in Canva, simply drag the image over the frame or use the upload option.

### Can I customize the frame style after adding an image?

Yes, you can customize the frame style by adjusting its size, color, or shape.

### How do I group frames in Canva?

To group frames, select them and use the group option.

### Can I use custom shapes as frames in Canva?

Yes, you can use custom shapes as frames by editing them in Canva or using external apps.

### Are Canva frames available on the mobile app?

Yes, frames are available and can be used in the Canva mobile app.
