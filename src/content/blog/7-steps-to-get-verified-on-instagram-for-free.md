Instagram verification is a coveted status symbol that adds credibility and authenticity to your profile. The blue checkmark next to your username signals that Instagram has confirmed you are who you say you are. While Instagram now offers paid verification through Meta Verified, it’s still possible to get verified for free if you meet certain criteria. This guide will walk you through the steps to apply for free Instagram verification and increase your chances of approval.

## What is Instagram Verification?

Instagram verification is the process by which Instagram confirms that an account is the authentic presence of the public figure, celebrity, or global brand it represents. Verified accounts display a blue checkmark badge next to their username, helping users find the genuine accounts of people and brands they want to follow.

**Benefits of being verified on Instagram include:**

- Increased credibility and trust with your audience

- Protection against impersonation

- Appearing higher in search results

- Access to exclusive features

Now let’s dive into the steps to apply for free Instagram verification.

## Step 1: Ensure Your Account Meets the Basic Requirements

Before applying, make sure your Instagram account meets these basic eligibility criteria:

- **Your account must be public**, Private accounts are not eligible for verification

- **Your profile must be complete**, This includes a bio, profile photo, and at least one post

- **Your account must represent a real person, registered business, or entity**

- **You must have a notable online presence**, Instagram looks for accounts that are featured in multiple news sources

Additionally, your account should follow Instagram’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.

## Step 2: Build Your Online Presence and Notability

Instagram states that verified accounts must be **notable** and represent a well-known, highly searched-for person or brand. While there’s no specific follower count required, having a strong online presence certainly helps. Here are some ways to increase your notability:

- Grow your following organically across social media platforms

- Get press coverage in reputable news outlets and publications

- Maintain an active, engaging Instagram account with quality content

- Build your presence on other platforms like YouTube, TikTok, or a personal website

The more established your online presence, the better your chances of verification approval.

## Step 3: Gather Documentation

To apply for verification, you’ll need to provide Instagram with proof of your identity. Acceptable forms of identification include:

- Government-issued photo ID (driver’s license, passport)

- Official business documents (tax filings, articles of incorporation)

- Recent utility bills

Make sure you have a digital copy of your ID ready before starting the application process.

## Step 4: Access the Verification Request Form

Now you’re ready to submit your verification request. Follow these steps:

1. Open the Instagram app and go to your profile

2. Tap the menu icon (three lines) in the top right corner

3. Select **Settings and Privacy**

4. Tap **Account**

5. Scroll down and select **Request Verification**

## Step 5: Fill Out the Verification Application

The verification form will ask for the following information:

- **Full Name:** Enter your legal name as it appears on your ID

- **Known As:** Your public name or the name your account represents

- **Category:** Select the category that best describes you (example: Blogger/Influencer, Business/Brand, etc.)

- **ID Upload:** Upload a photo of your government-issued ID or official business document

**Pro tip:** Be completely honest in your application. Providing false or misleading information can result in your request being denied and may even lead to account suspension.

## Step 6: Add Links to News Articles or Press

Instagram specifically looks for accounts that have been featured in multiple news sources when considering verification. In the application, you’ll have the opportunity to provide links that prove your notability.

Include links to:

- News articles featuring you or your brand

- Press releases

- Interviews

- Official websites that verify your identity

Avoid using links to social media profiles or paid/promotional content.

## Step 7: Submit Your Application and Wait

After filling out all required fields, tap **Submit**. Instagram will review your application and typically respond within 14 to 30 days. You’ll receive a notification in the app letting you know if your request was approved or denied.

If your request is denied, don’t be discouraged. You can reapply after 30 days. Use this time to further build your online presence and gather more evidence of your notability.

## Tips to Increase Your Chances of Verification

While there’s no guaranteed way to get verified, these additional tips can help improve your odds:

- **Be consistent across platforms:** Use the same profile photo, bio, and username across your social media accounts

- Post high-quality, original content regularly

- Engage with your audience through comments, direct messages, and Instagram Stories

- Avoid using third-party apps that promise verification (these are often scams)

- Report impersonator accounts to show Instagram there’s a need to verify your authentic presence

## What to Do If Your Verification Request Is Denied

If Instagram denies your verification request, don’t lose hope. Here are some steps to take:

1. Review Instagram’s guidelines to ensure you meet all criteria

2. Continue building your online presence and gathering press mentions

3. Improve your account’s authenticity and completeness

4. Wait 30 days before reapplying with updated information

Remember, many influential accounts aren’t verified, and you can still build a successful Instagram presence without the blue checkmark.

## Conclusion

Getting verified on Instagram for free is challenging but not impossible. By following these steps and consistently working to increase your online notability, you can improve your chances of receiving that coveted blue checkmark.

Remember that verification is just one aspect of building a successful Instagram presence. Focus on creating valuable content, engaging with your audience, and growing your following organically. With persistence and patience, you may eventually join the ranks of verified Instagram users.

Whether you get verified or not, the most important thing is to stay true to your brand and continue providing value to your followers. Good luck on your Instagram verification journey!
