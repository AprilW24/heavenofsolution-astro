Trying to view the followers of a private Instagram account can be tricky. Instagram’s privacy settings are designed to protect user privacy by limiting access to private profiles. However, there are a few legitimate methods you can try to reveal some of the followers of a private account.

**Here are the key things you need to know:**

- Private accounts only let approved followers see posts

- You can’t view the full list of followers unless you’re already following the private account

- There are some workarounds to reveal partial follower information

In this article, we’ll explore the options to know followers of private Instagram accounts without violating privacy policies.

### Steps

#### 1. Send a Follow Request

The most straightforward way is to simply send a follow request to the private account. If the user approves your request, you’ll gain access as a follower and can then view their full followers list. However, keep in mind the user may reject your request.

#### 2. Check for Mutual Followers

Even without access to the private profile, you can still see any mutual followers you share with that user. To do this:

- Go to the private profile page

- Tap **Followers** below their bio

- View the list of mutual followers

This reveals some of the accounts that follow them.

#### 3. Use an Instagram Viewer Website

There are websites like [Social Blade](https://socialblade.com/) that can reveal limited analytics on Instagram user profiles, including some follower data for private accounts.

After entering the private profile username, these sites show information like:

- Total followers

- Follower growth analysis

- Top locations of followers

While not the full list, it provides some insight.

### Conclusion

Trying to access the followers list of a private Instagram account without permission is tricky. Your options are limited without violating Instagram’s terms of service. Send a follow request, check for mutual followers, or use analytics sites to gain partial insights. However, respect other users’ privacy by not accessing their accounts without consent.
