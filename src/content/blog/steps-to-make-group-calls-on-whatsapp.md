Making group calls on WhatsApp is free and easy to set up from your Android device. With just a few taps, you can start a group call with up to 32 people. The call quality is usually very good, even for long conversations. This makes WhatsApp group calls useful for both personal and professional purposes.[](https://www.theverge.com/2020/4/9/21210345/whatsapp-group-call-how-to-video-audio-text-android-iphone)

In this simple guide, we will walk through the step-by-step process to start a WhatsApp group call from Android. Whether you want to chat with friends or have a team meeting, these tips will help you get set up in just minutes. Follow these straightforward steps to make group calls on WhatsApp from your Android phone or tablet.

1. Open the WhatsApp app on your Android device.

2. Scroll through your WhatsApp chats and tap on the group you want to call.

3. At the top right of the chat window, tap the phone icon to start a group call.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/WhatsApp-Steps-HoS.jpg)

4. Tap **Call group** at the bottom to confirm and start ringing everyone in the group.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/WhatsApp-Steps-HoS-2.jpg)

WhatsApp group calls provide a free, convenient way to connect multiple people on the go. With voice calling support, they can replace other group call chat apps for many purposes. Follow the steps above to easily start group voice calls from Android devices.

With smart group management and good connectivity, WhatsApp group calls offer crystal clear audio and smooth video for groups of up to 32. Give it a try for your next team huddle, family catch-up, or virtual hangout session with friends across the globe!
