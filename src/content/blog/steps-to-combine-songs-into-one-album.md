You can follow these steps if you want to combine songs into one album.

1. First, open your **File Manager**.

2. Then, click **Local files**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Combine-Songs-into-One-Album-on-Android1.jpeg)

3. After that, select storage for your album, **Device storage** or **SD card**.

4. Next, add a new folder by tapping **New**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Combine-Songs-into-One-Album-on-Android2.jpeg)

5. Then, write your album name, for example, ‘**Love Songs**,’ and click **OK**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Combine-Songs-into-One-Album-on-Android3.jpeg)

6. Next, choose your love music on the music folder and click **Copy**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Combine-Songs-into-One-Album-on-Android4.jpeg)

7. Then, **Paste** all music copied into the Love Songs album.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Combine-Songs-into-One-Album-on-Android5.jpeg)

8. Last, you can see your Love Songs album on your **Music app**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2022/02/Steps-to-Combine-Songs-into-One-Album-on-Android6.jpeg)
