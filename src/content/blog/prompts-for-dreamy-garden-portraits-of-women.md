If you love the look of soft, ethereal garden photos, Gemini AI can actually pull them off really well. 🌸 I’ve been testing a lot of text-to-image prompts lately, and garden portraits of women are some of the most satisfying results I’ve gotten. The trick is giving Gemini enough visual detail so it knows exactly what kind of light, mood, and setting you’re going for.

To use these prompts, just go to gemini.google.com and click “Create image” on the left side panel. Then paste any prompt below into the text box and hit enter. You don’t need to upload any photo since these prompts are written to generate original images entirely from text. ✨ Try tweaking small details like flower types or dress colors to make the result more personal.

### 1. Soft Morning Light Rose Garden Portrait with Ivory Silk Dress

*“A photorealistic portrait of a young woman standing in a full-bloom rose garden during early morning. She wears a flowing ivory silk dress with delicate lace details at the sleeves, her dark hair loosely pinned with a few strands falling naturally around her face. The light is soft and diffused, coming through morning mist from the east, casting gentle warm golden tones across her skin and the pale pink roses surrounding her. The roses fill the frame at various depths, from sharp foreground petals to soft blurred blooms in the background. Her expression is calm and peaceful, eyes slightly downcast with a soft smile. Shot with an 85mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, bokeh background of pastel roses, high resolution, cinematic quality, soft film grain.”*
