Viewing who has reshared your Instagram stories or posts can be useful to see how your content is resonating with your audience. Instagram makes this easy by providing options to see current public story reshares. Here are the step-by-step instructions to view story reshares on the Instagram app.

1. Open the Instagram app on your phone.

2. Go to your profile.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Instagram-Steps-HoS.jpg)

3. Tap on the post you want to check for reshares.

4. Tap on the three dots icon in the top right corner of the post.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Instagram-Steps-HoS-8.jpg)

5. Next, select **View insight**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Instagram-Steps-HoS-2-3.jpg)

6. Here you can see the Share icon to see who has publicly reshared your post to their Instagram Stories in the last 24 hours.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Instagram-Steps-HoS-3-2.jpg)

## Tips

- You can only see public story reshares, not private ones

- If no one has reshared your post publicly, the “View Story Reshares” option will not show up

- Story reshares are only visible for 24 hours after being shared

## Conclusion

Viewing public Instagram story reshares is easy by tapping the three dots icon on your post and selecting the “View insight” option. This shows you a grid of users who have reshared your post and lets you view their account. Use this to see who is engaging with your content.
