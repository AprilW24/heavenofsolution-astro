Instagram has become one of the most popular social media platforms, with over 1 billion monthly active users. With so many people using Instagram, finding a unique username that hasn’t already been taken can be difficult. Fortunately, Instagram allows you to change your username in case your current one is no longer suitable or available.

Your Instagram username is the name that comes after the “@” symbol when people tag or mention you in comments and captions (e.g. @yourusername). It’s an important part of your Instagram identity and brand.

There are a few reasons why you may want to change your Instagram username on your Android device:

- Your current username no longer reflects who you are or what you do.

- You want to create a more professional branding for your business or personal profile.

- Someone else has taken your desired username that better represents your brand.

- You simply want a new username that’s catchier and easier to remember.

The process of changing your Instagram username on Android only takes a minute. Follow the simple step-by-step instructions below to seamlessly change your username without losing any of your current followers or posts.

### Steps to Change Your Instagram Username

1. Open the Instagram app on your Android device. Tap on your profile icon in the bottom right corner.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Instagram-Steps-HoS.jpg)

2. Tap **Edit Profile**. This option is located underneath your bio and above your highlights.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Instagram-Steps-HoS-10.jpg)

3. Tap on the **Username** field. Delete your current username so you can type a new one.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Instagram-Steps-HoS-2-5.jpg)

4. Type your new desired username. You can use a mix of letters, numbers, periods, and underscores. Keep it under 30 characters.

5. Tap the check mark icon in the top right corner to save your new username.

If your desired username is taken, Instagram will notify you. You can then try variations by adding extra numbers or characters until you find a unique username.

And you’re done! Your new Instagram username on Android should now be updated. You can repeat this process as many times as you want, as long as your new username hasn’t already been claimed by another user.

### Conclusion

Changing your Instagram username on your Android device is a quick and easy process that only takes about a minute. Simply open your Instagram app, edit your profile, change your username in the designated field, and save your changes.

Having an Instagram username that represents your brand and personality is important for creating a cohesive social media presence. Take advantage of the ability to change it up whenever you please. Just make sure your new username is unique, catchy and reflects the real you!
