## How to Change Your Space Icon on Perplexity App for Android

Here’s the quick and easy way to update your Space icon on Android:

1. Open the *Perplexity* app on your Android device.

2. Tap the ***Spaces*** icon at the bottom.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/delete-a-Space-Perplexity.webp)

3. Find the Space you want to change and tap on it.

4. Tap the three-dot menu in the top right corner.

5. Choose ***Edit Space*** from the menu.

6. Tap the current icon or emoji next to your Space’s name.

7. Pick a new emoji or icon that fits your Space’s vibe.

8. Hit ***Update*** to lock in your changes.

9. Double-check your Space list to see your new icon in action! 🎨

## FAQs

### How do I create a new Space on Perplexity Android?

Tap the *Library* icon, go to *Spaces*, and hit the + icon to start a new Space.

### Can I use any emoji as my Space icon?

Yep, you can pick any emoji that fits your Space’s mood or topic.

### Do I need a Pro account to change Space icons?

Most customization features are available to all users, but some advanced options may need *Perplexity Pro*.

### What if I accidentally pick the wrong icon?

Just go back to *Edit Space* and pick a new one—no stress.

### Can I change the Space icon from my computer?

Yes, you can update your Space icon from the web version, and it’ll sync to your app.
