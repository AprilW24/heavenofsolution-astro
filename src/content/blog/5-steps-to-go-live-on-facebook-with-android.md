Facebook Live is a powerful tool that allows you to connect with your audience in real-time. Introduced in 2015, it quickly became popular among individuals and businesses alike, offering a unique opportunity for genuine interaction and engagement. This article will guide you through the steps to go live on Facebook using your Android device. Following are the steps.

1. Start by opening the Facebook app on your Android device.

2. Navigate to the **What’s on your mind?** section at the top of your screen.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/5-Steps-to-Go-Live-on-Facebook-with-Android.jpg)

3. In the new post options, select **Live Video**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/5-Steps-to-Go-Live-on-Facebook-with-Android-2.jpg)

4. Before you start your live video, it’s important to give it a name and description. This will let your viewers know what your livestream is about. You can also tag friends, add location information, or a feeling/activity.

5. Once you’ve set everything up, click the **Go Live** button to start your live stream.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/5-Steps-to-Go-Live-on-Facebook-with-Android-3.jpg)

Going live on Facebook with your Android device is a straightforward process that allows you to connect with your audience in real-time. Whether you’re sharing a special moment, hosting a Q&A, or teaching a class, Facebook Live offers a unique and engaging way to communicate. Remember to prepare your content, engage with your viewers during the livestream, and have fun!
