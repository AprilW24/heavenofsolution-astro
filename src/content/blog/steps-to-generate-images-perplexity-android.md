## How the Perplexity App Works with Image Generation

If you’re on Android, launching the Perplexity app might trick you into thinking image creation isn’t possible. The thing is, direct image generation usually isn’t available inside the app itself—at least for now. Users (myself included) often report that the feature just doesn’t show up, even for Pro subscribers. So if you searched everywhere and felt a little lost, you’re not crazy.

That said, Perplexity hasn’t left us totally out in the cold. The actual image generation tech is right at your fingertips—it just lives in the mobile web version. If you can access Perplexity through a regular browser like Chrome, you’ll have the same image-making powers you’d find on a desktop or iPhone. The Android app might catch up soon, but for now, it’s the browser route that gets stuff done.

## Step-by-Step to Generate Images with Perplexity on Your Android

Ready to roll? Here’s everything you need, in just a few taps:

1. Open Chrome (or your browser of choice) on your Android device.

2. Go to *perplexity.ai* and log in to your account.

3. Close any pop-up that tries to redirect you to the Android app.

4. Start a new chat or thread as you usually would.

5. Type your prompt, like “generate an image of a cat surfing” (get creative!).

6. Send the prompt and wait for Perplexity to respond.

7. If available, look for the “Generate Image” section or button in the response thread.

8. Tap to customize the image style (choose from illustration, photo, painting, diagram, etc.).

9. Submit your choices and let Perplexity work its magic.

10. Download, share, or tweak the AI-generated image right from your phone.

## Picking the Right Style and Model for Your AI Images

One bonus you’ll find on the web version is the power to fine-tune your image. Want a *minimalist* touch or that *watercolor* vibe? The browser lets you pick styles, so your results feel way more “you.” If your prompt isn’t hitting just right, you can always adjust and generate again.

What makes it cooler? You can even pick the AI model used for generating the image. Under account preferences, there are options like GPT Image 1, *DALL-E 3*, FLUX.1, or *Gemini 2.0 Flash*. Each has its own flavor and capabilities. It’s all about finding what suits your needs, whether you’re a free user (with image limits) or a Pro subscriber craving unlimited spins.

## FAQs

### Can I generate images directly in the Perplexity Android app?

No, the current app doesn’t support it—you’ll need to use the mobile browser version.

### Do I need a Pro subscription to make images?

You can try for free, but subscribers get more daily and monthly image requests. Free accounts have limits.

### What kind of images can I make?

Whatever you can describe in a prompt. Think photos, drawings, diagrams, or even custom styles. Just be specific with your instructions.

### Can I use these images for work projects?

Nope, Perplexity’s images are for personal use only—not for commercial projects.

### What models are available for image creation?

You can pick from GPT Image 1, *DALL-E 3*, FLUX.1, or *Gemini 2.0 Flash* by tweaking your settings in the browser.
