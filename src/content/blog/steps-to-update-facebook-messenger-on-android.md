Keeping apps updated on your Android device is important to get the latest features and security updates. Facebook Messenger is used by billions of people around the world to stay connected with friends and family. In this article, we will walk you through the simple steps to update Facebook Messenger to the latest version on your Android device.

Updating Facebook Messenger only takes a few minutes. Follow these steps:

1. Open the Play Store app. The Play Store is where you can update and download apps on your Android device. You can find it in your list of apps.

2. At the top right corner of the Play Store app, you will see your profile icon. Tap on it.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Play-Store-Steps-HoS.jpg)

3. A menu will open. Choose the **Manage apps & device** option.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Play-Store-Steps-HoS-2.jpg)

4. Next select **Updates available**, and you will see a list of apps that have updates pending. Look for the Facebook Messenger app in the list.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/01/Play-Store-Steps-HoS-3.jpg)

5. If Messenger is in the list, simply tap the **Update** button next to it to update the app to the latest version.

The update process will now start. Wait for it to complete. Once finished, you will have the latest version of Facebook Messenger on your Android device.

### Conclusion

Updating apps regularly keeps them running smoothly and securely. Follow the simple steps outlined above to update Facebook Messenger on your Android device. Keep the app updated to get all the new features and fixes that come with each version. An updated Messenger app will help you stay effortlessly connected with your friends and family.
