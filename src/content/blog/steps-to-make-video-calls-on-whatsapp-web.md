WhatsApp is one of the most popular messaging apps in the world, allowing users to easily communicate with friends, family, and colleagues through text, voice, and video. While most people are familiar with using WhatsApp on their smartphones, did you know that you can also use WhatsApp Web to make video calls from your computer? In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to make video calls on WhatsApp Web using your Android phone.

## Steps to Make a Video Call on WhatsApp Web with Android

### Step 1: Set Up WhatsApp Web

To get started, you’ll need to set up WhatsApp Web on your computer. Here’s how:

1. Open your web browser and go to https://web.whatsapp.com/

2. On your Android phone, open WhatsApp and tap the three-dot menu icon

3. Select **Linked Devices** and then tap **Link a Device**

4. Use your phone to scan the QR code displayed on the WhatsApp Web page

5. WhatsApp Web will now be connected to your phone and you’ll see your chats and contacts

### Step 2: Select a Contact

Once you have WhatsApp Web set up, you can start making video calls. Here’s how:

1. In the chat list on the left side of the screen, select the contact you want to video call

2. Click on the video camera icon in the top right corner of the chat window

3. WhatsApp will ask for permission to use your computer’s camera and microphone. Click **Allow**

4. The video call will start ringing on your contact’s phone

### Step 3: During the Video Call

During the video call, you’ll see your contact’s video feed on the main part of the screen, with your own video in a smaller window in the corner. Here are some things you can do during the call:

- **Mute/Unmute:** Click the microphone icon to mute or unmute your audio

- **Turn Video On/Off:** Click the camera icon to turn your video on or off

- **Switch Camera:** Click the switch camera icon to switch between your computer’s front and rear cameras (if available)

- **End Call:** Click the red phone icon to end the call

### Step 4: Group Video Calls

You can also make group video calls on WhatsApp Web with up to 8 participants. Here’s how:

1. In the chat list, select the group you want to video call

2. Click the video camera icon in the top right corner

3. Select the contacts you want to add to the call (up to 7 others)

4. Click the video camera icon again to start the call

The group video call will work similarly to a one-on-one call, with everyone’s video feeds shown on the screen.

## Conclusion

WhatsApp Web makes it easy to stay connected with your contacts even when you’re away from your phone. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can quickly and easily make video calls right from your computer using your Android phone. Whether you’re catching up with friends, collaborating with colleagues, or staying in touch with family, WhatsApp Web video calls are a convenient and reliable way to communicate.

So next time you need to make a video call, give WhatsApp Web a try, you might be surprised at how simple and effective it is! Just remember to make sure you have a stable internet connection for the best video and audio quality.
