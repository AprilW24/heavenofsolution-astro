Creating cinematic island photos used to mean booking a flight and hiring a professional photographer. With Gemini AI, you can generate stunning, movie-quality island shots for both women and men right from your screen. 🌊 These prompts are built for pure creation with no reference photos needed.

To use these, go to gemini.google.com or open the Gemini app, then find the image generation feature powered by *Nano Banana Pro*. 🎨 Paste any prompt straight into the chat and send it. Feel free to swap things like outfit color, skin tone, or lighting mood before you hit generate.

She wears a flowing off-white linen dress, her long hair gently blowing in the warm ocean wind. The sun sits low on the horizon behind her, casting a strong golden rim light around her silhouette. Lush tropical greenery frames the left side of the frame. Crystal-clear turquoise water stretches to the horizon. Shot on a Sony A7R V, 35mm f/1.8 lens, shallow depth of field, soft background bokeh, warm cinematic color grade with deep shadow contrast, subtle 35mm film grain, ultra-realistic photography, 8K resolution.”*
