Facebook Messenger is a popular messaging platform used by billions of people around the world. It offers features like text messaging, group chats, video calls, and more. One useful but lesser known Messenger feature is the ability to share audio files like music or podcasts.

Sharing audio on Messenger is easy and allows you to instantly send your favorite songs, audio messages, or other sound clips to friends and family. Whether you want to share a new track from your favorite band or send an important voice message, Messenger makes it simple.

In this guide, we will cover three simple methods to share audio files on Facebook Messenger:

- Using file sharing services like Google Drive or Dropbox

- Converting audio to video

- Sending shorter 1-minute audio messages

Follow these steps, and you’ll be able to share audio seamlessly on Messenger, even if the clips are longer than 1 minute.

#### Steps to Share Audio on Messenger

1. Use File Sharing Services

The easiest way to share long audio is by using a file hosting service like Google Drive or Dropbox.

Follow these steps:

- Upload your audio file (MP3, WAV, etc.) to Google Drive or Dropbox

- Get a shareable link for the file

- Paste the link in your Messenger chat

- The recipient can then stream or download the audio

This method works for audio of any length. Services like Google Drive will even stream the audio directly in Messenger, allowing your friends to listen without downloading.

2. Convert Audio to Video

Another excellent way to share audio is to convert it into a shareable video file. Services like Audiogram make this conversion process simple.

Here is how to convert audio to video:

- Go to [Audiogram](https://audiogram.com/) and upload your audio file

- Audiogram will automatically convert the audio into a stylish, shareable video

- Download the generated video to your device

- Share the video directly on Messenger as you would any other video

The video file will include the audio waveform visualization and can be shared seamlessly. This method makes it easy to post longer audio content on Messenger.

3. Send 1-Minute Audio Messages

If you only need to share short audio clips, you can easily record them directly in Messenger without any extra steps.

To send a 1-minute audio recording:

- Open your Messenger chat

- Press and hold the mic icon to start recording

- Record your message (up to 1 minute)

- Release your finger to stop recording

- Press send!

The 1-minute recording limit allows you to quickly capture voice messages, songs, or other short audio clips. While you can’t directly share longer files this way, it’s the easiest method for quick sound bites.

#### Conclusion

Sharing audio files on Facebook Messenger is simple once you know the various workarounds for longer audio clips.

Use online file sharing platforms to send audio of any length or convert your audio into video for an elegant, streamlined sharing experience. For quick sound bites under 1 minute, use Messenger’s built-in voice messaging feature.

With these tips, you can easily send your favorite tunes, audio messages, podcast clips and more on Messenger. Share awesome audio moments with all your friends and family today.
