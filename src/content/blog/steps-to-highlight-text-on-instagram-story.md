Instagram Stories are a fantastic way to engage with your followers and share snippets of your day-to-day life. One great feature is the ability to highlight text in your stories, making certain words or phrases stand out. Highlighting text can help emphasize important information, add visual interest, or simply make your story more eye-catching. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to highlight text on your Instagram story.

## Step 1: Create a New Instagram Story

![](/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/HoS_HighlightText.jpg)

To get started, open the Instagram app and tap on your profile picture in the upper left corner to create a new story. You can either take a new photo or video, or select one from your camera roll.

## Step 2: Add Text to Your Story

Once you have your photo or video selected, tap the **Aa** icon at the top right of the screen to add text. Type out your desired text using the keyboard. You can change the font, color, and size of your text using the options at the top of the screen.

## Step 3: Highlight Your Text

To highlight your text, tap the **A** icon with stars on either side, located to the left of the color palette at the top of the screen. This will automatically highlight your text with a **default color**, usually white text on a black background.

If you want to change the highlight color, simply tap the color palette icon and select your desired color. The background color of your highlighted text will change to the selected color, while the text itself will adjust to a contrasting color for readability.

## Step 4: Adjust the Highlight

If you want to make further adjustments to your highlighted text, you can use the slider bar at the bottom of the screen to change the text alignment. You can also pinch and zoom to resize the text box, or use two fingers to rotate it.

## Step 5: Add More Text or Elements

Feel free to add more text boxes and experiment with different highlight colors for each one. You can also add stickers, gifs, or drawings to your story to make it even more engaging.

## Step 6: Share Your Story

Once you’re happy with your highlighted text, tap **Send To >** to share your story. You can choose to share it with all of your followers, or select specific people or groups.

## Tips for Highlighting Text Effectively

- **Keep it short and sweet**: Highlighted text should be used sparingly to emphasize key points or phrases. Don’t highlight every word or your story will look cluttered.

- **Choose colors wisely**: Make sure your highlight color contrasts well with the text color for maximum readability. Bright colors can be eye-catching but don’t overdo it.

- **Use highlights to guide the eye**: Position your highlighted text strategically to draw attention to important information or calls-to-action.

- **Combine with other elements**: Pair your highlighted text with relevant stickers, emojis, or drawings to reinforce your message and add visual interest.

## Conclusion

Highlighting text on your Instagram story is a simple yet effective way to make your content more engaging and impactful. By following these easy steps and tips, you can create stories that grab attention and communicate your message clearly. So go ahead and experiment with different highlight colors and styles to see what works best for your brand or personal aesthetic. With a little creativity, you can take your Instagram stories to the next level!
