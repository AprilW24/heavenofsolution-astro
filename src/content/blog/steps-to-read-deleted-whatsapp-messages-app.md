WhatsApp’s **Delete for Everyone** feature, introduced in 2017, allows users to delete messages within a specific time frame. While this feature is useful for retracting mistakes or sensitive information, it can be frustrating for recipients who are left wondering about the content of the deleted message. This article explores different methods and apps that can help you read deleted WhatsApp messages, ensuring you never miss out on important information.

### Steps to Read Deleted WhatsApp Messages

#### 1. **Using Notification History on Android**

One of the simplest ways to read deleted WhatsApp messages on Android is by using the built-in notification history feature. This method is available on devices running Android 11 or later.

**Steps:**

- **Open Settings:** Go to your device’s settings.

- **Navigate to Notifications:** Scroll down and tap on **Apps & Notifications.**

- **Access Notification History:** Tap on **Notifications** and then **Notification history.**

- **Enable Notification History:** Toggle the button next to **Use notification history** to turn it on.

Once enabled, this feature will keep a log of all your notifications, including WhatsApp messages, even if they are deleted by the sender. You can access this log to read the deleted messages.

#### 2. **Using Third-Party Apps**

Several third-party apps are designed to help you recover and read deleted WhatsApp messages. While these apps can be effective, it’s important to exercise caution as they may pose security risks. Here are a few popular options:

**a. WAMR**

WAMR is a popular app that allows you to recover deleted messages and media from WhatsApp.

**Steps:**

- **Download WAMR:** Install the app from the Google Play Store.

- **Grant Permissions:** Open the app and grant the necessary permissions, including notification access and media access.

- **Enable Notification Access:** Follow the on-screen instructions to enable notification access for WAMR.

- **Recover Messages:** Once set up, WAMR will start logging your notifications. You can view deleted messages and media within the app.

**b. Notisave**

Notisave is another app that keeps track of your notifications, allowing you to read deleted WhatsApp messages.

**Steps:**

- **Download Notisave:** Install the app from the Google Play Store.

- **Grant Permissions:** Open the app and grant the necessary permissions, including notification access.

- **Enable Auto-Start:** Ensure the app is set to auto-start to keep logging notifications.

- **View Deleted Messages:** Open Notisave to view deleted WhatsApp messages from the notification log.

**c. Get Deleted Messages**

This app is specifically designed to recover deleted WhatsApp messages.

**Steps:**

- **Download the App:** Install **Get Deleted Messages** from the Google Play Store.

- **Grant Permissions:** Open the app and grant the necessary permissions.

- **Check Deleted Messages:** Whenever a message is deleted on WhatsApp, open the app to check the deleted message.

#### 3. **Restoring from Backup**

If you regularly back up your WhatsApp chats, you can restore deleted messages from a previous backup. This method works for both Android and iOS devices.

**Steps for Android:**

- **Uninstall WhatsApp:** Remove the app from your device.

- **Reinstall WhatsApp:** Download and reinstall WhatsApp from the Google Play Store.

- **Restore from Backup:** During the setup process, you will be prompted to restore your chat history from Google Drive. Select the backup that contains the deleted messages.

**Steps for iOS:**

- **Uninstall WhatsApp:** Remove the app from your device.

- **Reinstall WhatsApp:** Download and reinstall WhatsApp from the App Store.

- **Restore from iCloud:** During the setup process, you will be prompted to restore your chat history from iCloud. Select the backup that contains the deleted messages.

### Conclusion

Reading deleted WhatsApp messages is possible through various methods, including using notification history, third-party apps, and restoring from backups. While these methods can be effective, it’s important to consider the potential security risks associated with third-party apps. Always ensure you download apps from reputable sources and grant permissions cautiously.

By following the steps outlined in this article, you can stay informed and never miss out on important information, even if the sender deletes the message. Whether you choose to use built-in features or third-party apps, recovering deleted WhatsApp messages has never been easier.
