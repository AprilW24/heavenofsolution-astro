Snapchat is a popular social media app known for its fun filters, lenses, and disappearing messages. One lesser-known feature is the ability to customize your chat colors, giving your conversations a personalized touch. In this article, we’ll walk you through the steps to get different colors on Snapchat for Android devices.

Before we dive into the steps, make sure you have the following:

- An Android device with the latest version of Snapchat installed

- A Snapchat+ subscription (required for custom chat colors)

## Step 1: Open a Chat

To begin, open the Snapchat app and navigate to the chat you want to customize. This can be a one-on-one conversation or a group chat. Tap on the chat to open it.

## Step 2: Access Chat Settings

Once in the chat, tap on the friend’s or group’s name at the top of the screen. This will open the Friendship Profile or Group Profile.

## Step 3: Select Chat Color

Under the **Our Chat** section, look for the **Chat Color** option and tap on it. This will open a color picker where you can choose from a variety of hues.

## Step 4: Choose Your Desired Color

Browse through the available colors and tap on the one you like. You can preview how the color will look in the chat background.

## Step 5: Save Your Changes

After selecting your preferred color, tap the **Save** button to apply the changes. The chat background will now display the chosen color.

## Additional Customization Options

Snapchat offers several other ways to personalize your experience:

- *Custom App Icons*: Snapchat+ subscribers can change the app icon to one of several themed options. Go to your Profile, tap the Snapchat+ membership card, select **App Icon**, and choose your desired icon.

- *Bitmoji Reactions*: Express yourself with emoji reactions to messages. You can also respond with your personalized Bitmoji avatar.

- *Map Reactions*: If you’ve shared your location with friends, you can send quick reactions like hearts or waves when you see them on the Snap Map.

## Upcoming Features

Snapchat is constantly evolving and introducing new features. Some exciting updates to look forward to include:

- *Message Editing*: Soon, you’ll be able to edit sent messages within a 5-minute window, as long as the recipient hasn’t replied yet. This feature will initially roll out to Snapchat+ subscribers.

- *AI-Generated Bitmoji Clothing*: Describe your desired outfit, and Snapchat’s AI chatbot will generate unique digital clothes for your Bitmoji based on your text input.

- *AI Reminders*: Set reminders for important events by chatting with Snapchat’s AI assistant, which will create an in-app countdown.

## Conclusion

Customizing chat colors on Snapchat for Android is a simple way to add a personal touch to your conversations. With a Snapchat+ subscription, you can easily change the background color of your chats in just a few taps. Additionally, Snapchat offers various other customization options and exciting upcoming features powered by artificial intelligence. By following the steps outlined in this article and staying up-to-date with the latest updates, you can enhance your Snapchat experience and make it truly your own.
