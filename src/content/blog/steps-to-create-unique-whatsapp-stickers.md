Sending the same sticker packs over and over gets old real fast. 😅 If you’ve been using the default sticker packs that come with WhatsApp, you’re missing out on something way more personal and fun. Custom stickers let you put your own face, your pet, your friends, or literally any image you love right into your chat bubbles.

The good news is that you don’t need to be a designer to pull this off. 🎨 WhatsApp actually has a built-in sticker creator that works with just your phone’s camera roll. No extra tools needed, no complicated software to figure out. It’s one of those features most people scroll past without ever trying it.

In this guide, I’m going to walk you through how to make your own WhatsApp stickers from scratch, what apps can help you go even further, and some tips to keep your stickers looking clean and sharp. Whether you’re on Android or iPhone, this works for both. 📱

## Why Custom WhatsApp Stickers Hit Different Than the Default Ones

There’s something about sending a sticker of your own face with a funny expression that a generic cartoon just can’t replace. 😂 Custom stickers feel personal because they are personal. They carry inside jokes, your actual personality, and moments that only make sense to the people you’re chatting with. That’s the kind of thing that makes a group chat actually fun instead of just functional.

Custom stickers also work great for branding if you run a small business or a content page. Imagine sending clients or followers a sticker pack that has your logo, your mascot, or your signature catchphrase on it. It’s a small touch that people actually remember. And since WhatsApp is one of the most used messaging apps globally, your stickers can travel fast through shared chats.

The best part is that once you build your sticker packs, they stay saved in your WhatsApp. You don’t have to recreate them every time you want to use them. 🙌 They sit right there in your sticker tab, ready to drop into any conversation whenever the moment is right.

## How to Make a WhatsApp Sticker Using the Built-In Create Tool

This is the fastest way to get your first custom sticker made, and you don’t need to download anything extra. WhatsApp has had this feature for a while now, but a lot of people still don’t know it’s there. Here’s how to do it step by step 👇

1. Open WhatsApp and go into any chat, whether it’s a personal chat or a group.

2. Tap the emoji icon at the bottom left of the text input bar.

3. Swipe to the sticker tab, which looks like a small square sticker icon.

4. Tap the “+” or “**Create**” button that appears at the top or bottom of the sticker panel.

5. WhatsApp will open your photo gallery so you can pick any image you want to turn into a sticker.

6. Select the photo you want to use, such as a selfie, a picture of your pet, or a funny screenshot.

7. WhatsApp will automatically cut out the main subject from the background using its built-in *AI* detection.

8. Adjust the cutout if needed by pinching, zooming, or repositioning the subject on the sticker canvas.

9. Add text, doodles, or emojis on top of the image using the editing tools on screen.

10. Tap “**Done**” or “**Add to Pack**” to save your sticker, then send it right away or store it for later use.

## Best Third-Party Apps That Give You More Control Over Your Stickers 🛠️

WhatsApp’s built-in tool is great for quick stickers, but if you want more control over the final look, third-party apps are the way to go. Apps like *Sticker.ly*, *Sticker Maker*, and *Sticker Maker Studio* give you full editing tools including manual background removal, text styling, borders, and even animated sticker support. They’re free to download on both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store, and they all have a one-tap “Add to WhatsApp” button when your pack is ready.

*Sticker.ly* is especially good if you want animated stickers, since it lets you turn short video clips or *GIF* files into moving stickers. You just upload your clip, trim it, remove the background if you want, and export it straight to WhatsApp. 🎬 *Canva* is another solid option if you want to design stickers from scratch using templates, fonts, and graphics, then download them as *PNG* files with transparent backgrounds to import into a sticker app.

One thing to keep in mind is that WhatsApp has specific requirements for stickers. Each sticker needs to be under 100KB in size and should ideally have a transparent background saved as a *PNG* file. 📐 Most sticker apps handle this automatically, but if you’re designing from scratch in *Canva* or another tool, make sure you export correctly before importing into WhatsApp or a sticker-maker app.

## FAQs

### Can I make WhatsApp stickers without downloading any app?

Yes! WhatsApp has a built-in sticker creator that lets you turn any photo from your gallery into a sticker right inside the app without needing anything extra.

### Do WhatsApp stickers work on both Android and iPhone?

Absolutely. 📱 The built-in sticker creation feature is available on both Android and iOS versions of WhatsApp, as long as your app is updated to a recent version.

### What image format works best for custom WhatsApp stickers?

*PNG* files with a transparent background work best because they give your sticker a clean cutout look without a white or colored box around the subject.

### Can I share my custom sticker pack with friends?

Right now, WhatsApp doesn’t have a built-in pack-sharing feature, but if you use a third-party app like *Sticker.ly*, you can share your pack via a link that friends can add to their own WhatsApp.

### How many stickers can I add to one pack?

WhatsApp allows a minimum of 3 stickers and a maximum of 30 stickers per pack, so you have plenty of room to build a solid collection before starting a new one. 🎨
