## Steps to Convert Video to Images

### Step 1: Choose a Video-to-Image Tool

First, you need a tool that can extract frames from your video. There are several options available, including online tools like **ClipConverter** or **Online-Convert**, and software like **Adobe Premiere Pro** or **Shotcut**.

- **ClipConverter**: This is a free online tool that allows you to convert videos into various formats, including extracting frames as images.

- **Adobe Premiere Pro**: A professional video editing software that can be used to export video frames as images.

### Step 2: Extract Frames from Video

If using an online tool like ClipConverter:

1. Go to the ClipConverter website.

2. Upload your video file.

3. Choose the output format as images (e.g., JPEG).

4. Adjust settings like frame rate or interval if needed.

5. Download the extracted images.

If using Adobe Premiere Pro:

1. Open your video in Premiere Pro.

2. Go to **File** > **Export** > **Media**.

3. In the export settings, choose **Image** as the output format.

4. Set the frame rate or interval for exporting frames.

5. Export the frames.

### Step 3: Import Images into Canva

Once you have your images, you can import them into Canva for further editing:

1. Open Canva and create a new project.

2. Click on **Uploads** and select the images you extracted.

3. Use Canva𠏋 editing tools to adjust, resize, or enhance your images as needed.
