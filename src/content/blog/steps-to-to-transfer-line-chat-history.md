LINE is one of the most popular messaging apps, with over 700 million users worldwide. It allows you to send text messages, make voice and video calls, share photos and videos, and much more. However, when you switch to a new phone, your LINE chat history doesn’t automatically transfer over.

Fortunately, LINE provides a built-in feature to backup and restore your chat history. By following a few simple steps, you can ensure that all your important conversations, media files, and other data are safely transferred to your new device.

## Step 1: Back Up Your LINE Chat History

The first step in transferring your LINE chat history is to create a backup. Here’s how to do it:

- Open the LINE app on your old phone.

- Go to **Settings** > **Chats** > **Backup and restore chat history**.

- Tap on **Back up to Google Drive** (for Android) or **Back up now** (for iOS).

- Wait for the backup process to complete. This may take a few minutes depending on the size of your chat history.

## Step 2: Install LINE on Your New Phone

Once you have backed up your LINE chat history, the next step is to install the LINE app on your new phone. You can download it from the Google Play Store (for Android) or the App Store (for iOS).

## Step 3: Log In to Your LINE Account

After installing LINE on your new phone, log in to your account using the same phone number or email address that you used on your old device. If you have enabled two-factor authentication, you will need to enter the verification code sent to your registered email or phone number.

## Step 4: Restore Your LINE Chat History

Now that you are logged in to your LINE account on your new phone, it’s time to restore your chat history from the backup you created earlier. Here’s how:

- Go to **Settings** > **Chats** > **Backup and restore chat history**.

- Tap on **Restore from Google Drive** (for Android) or **Restore chat history** (for iOS).

- Select the backup file you want to restore and tap **Restore**.

- Wait for the restoration process to complete. This may take a few minutes depending on the size of your backup.

## Step 5: Verify Your Restored Chat History

After the restoration process is complete, open your LINE app and check if all your chats, media files, and other data have been successfully transferred to your new phone. You should be able to see all your previous conversations and pick up right where you left off.

## Conclusion

Transferring your LINE chat history to a new phone is a straightforward process that can be completed in just a few steps. By backing up your chat history and restoring it on your new device, you can ensure that you don’t lose any important conversations or media files when switching phones.

Remember to regularly backup your LINE chat history to avoid losing any data in case of unexpected issues like device loss, damage, or malfunction. With these simple steps, you can enjoy seamless messaging across all your devices and never worry about losing your precious memories again.
