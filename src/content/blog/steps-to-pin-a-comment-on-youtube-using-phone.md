Have you ever wanted to highlight an insightful comment on one of your YouTube videos? Pinning a comment is a great way to showcase important information, give a shout-out to a top fan, or provide additional context right at the top of the comments section.

As a YouTube creator, you can easily pin any comment on your own videos, whether it’s a comment you posted yourself or one from a viewer. When you pin a comment, it will appear at the very top of the comments section, making it the first thing people see.

In this guide, we’ll walk you through the simple steps to pin a comment on YouTube using the mobile app on your phone. Let’s get started!

### Step 1: Open the YouTube App and Go to Your Video

First, open the YouTube app on your iPhone or Android phone. If you’re not already signed in, make sure to sign in to your YouTube account.

Next, navigate to the video that has the comment you want to pin. The easiest way is to tap on your profile picture icon, then tap **Your Channel**. Find the video in your list of uploads and tap on it to open the video page.

### Step 2: Scroll Down to the Comments Section

Once the video starts playing, tap anywhere on the video to bring up the video controls and info. Look for the comments button below the video title, it will say something like **123 Comments**. Tap on this to scroll down to the comments section.

All the comments on the video will load below, with the newest comments first by default. Take a moment to browse through and find the comment you want to pin to the top. This could be a comment you previously posted yourself, or a comment from a viewer that you want to highlight.

### Step 3: Tap the Three Dots Icon and Select “Pin”

When you find the desired comment to pin, look for the three vertical dots icon on the right side of the comment. Tap on those three dots to bring up the comment options menu.

The menu will give you a few choices:

- Pin

- Remove

- Report

Simply tap **Pin** to select that option. You’ll get one more pop-up message asking you to confirm, noting that **If you’ve already pinned a comment, this will replace it**. Tap **Pin** again to confirm your choice.

That’s it! The comment will now have a **Pinned** label and be moved to the very top of the comments section, above all the other comments. It will stay pinned there unless you decide to unpin it or pin a different comment later.

### Some Additional Tips

Here are a few more things to keep in mind about pinning comments on YouTube from your phone:

You can only pin comments on your own videos. YouTube only allows creators to pin comments on their own video uploads. If you’re viewing a video from another channel, you won’t see the option to pin comments, even if you posted a comment yourself.

You can pin comments from yourself or viewers. Any comment on your video is eligible to be pinned, whether it’s a comment you posted or one from a viewer. However, only you as the channel owner have the ability to pin or unpin comments.

Only one comment can be pinned at a time. You can only have a single comment pinned on a video. If you pin a new comment, it will replace the previously pinned comment. The old pinned comment will return to its original spot in the comments section.

Pinned comments can be unpinned. If you change your mind and want to unpin a comment, simply tap the three dots and select **Unpin**. The comment will be unpinned and moved back to its original place in the chronological list of comments.

Pinned comments are public. Keep in mind that when you pin a comment, all of your viewers can see that you pinned it. Make sure you only pin comments that you’re comfortable showcasing to your entire audience.
