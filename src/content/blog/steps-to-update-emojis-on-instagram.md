Emojis have become an essential way for people to express themselves on social media platforms like Instagram. However, the default emoji set on Android devices can become outdated as new emojis are released every year. Thankfully, updating your emojis on Instagram for Android is a simple process.

Here are the key things you need to know:

- New emojis are typically released once per year. Keep an eye out for announcements from the Unicode Consortium.

- Updating your Android OS or keyboard app usually adds new emojis automatically.

- You can also install custom emoji apps to get new emoji packs.

### Steps to Update Emojis on Instagram for Android

**1. Check if your Android OS is up to date.**

New Android versions add the latest emojis. Go to Settings > System > System update to see if an update is available and install it.

**2. Update your keyboard app.**

Popular Android keyboards like Gboard also add new emojis regularly via app updates. Go to the Play Store, search for your keyboard app, and update it if an update is available.

**3. Install a custom emoji app.**

Apps like Bitmoji or Emojidom allow you to install emoji packs beyond the standard Android emoji set. These apps will display their emoji options directly on your Android keyboard.

Some great emoji apps to try are:

- **Bitmoji:** Create a personalized avatar and access fun cartoon emoji stickers.

- **Emojidom:** Provides monthly emoji updates with diverse skin tones.

- **Emoji Keyboard:** Offers thousands of high-quality emoji in different styles.

**4. Activate emoji keyboard.**

If you don’t see emoji options on your Android keyboard, go to your device Settings > System > Languages & input > Virtual keyboard > check **Show emoji switch key** option.

**5. Use emoji text combinations.**

Try typing **:** plus a relevant word like **smile** to view suggested emoji. The suggestions will improve over time as you use them.

### Conclusion

Updating the emojis on your Instagram for Android is easy and ensures you always have access to the latest emoji releases. Keep your Android OS version and keyboard app updated. Install custom emoji apps for even more emoji options. With a huge range of emojis at your fingertips, you can express yourself creatively on Instagram and stand out from the crowd!
