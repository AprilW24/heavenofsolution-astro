Calendars on our phones are extremely important for keeping track of events, appointments, birthdays, and other important dates. However, sometimes calendar data can get deleted or lost due to issues like accidental deletion, OS updates, switching phones, etc. Losing calendar data can be frustrating as you may miss out on events or appointments.

The good news is – with the right steps, you can easily restore your calendar on an Android phone. This article provides step-by-step instructions to restore your calendar using different methods.

### Method 1: Restore from Google Calendar Trash

If you use the Google Calendar app, it has a trash feature where deleted events are stored for 30 days before being permanently deleted. Here are the steps to restore calendar events from Google Calendar trash:

- Open the Google Calendar app on your Android phone

- Tap on the menu icon in the top left corner

- Select **Trash**

- Tap **Edit** and select the calendar events you want to restore

- Tap **Restore** to restore the selected events back to your calendar

This method allows you to easily restore events within 30 days of deletion.

### Method 2: Restore from Google Drive Data Backup

If you have Google Drive backup enabled, you can restore calendar data from the backup files:

- Install Google Drive on your computer

- Sign in to your Google Account

- Click on **Backups** in the left sidebar

- Locate the backup when your calendar data was available

- Right click on the backup and choose **Restore**

- Select the Calendar data and click **Restore**

This will restore your calendar backup to your Google Account. It may take some time to sync back to your Android phone.

### Method 4: Sync Calendar from Your Gmail

If you have added your Gmail account in Google Calendar, it automatically syncs calendar events from Gmail. You can enable this sync to restore missing events:

- Open Google Calendar settings

- Go to **Events from Gmail**

- Turn on the feature and sync events from Gmail

### Conclusion

Losing calendar data on your Android phone can be frustrating. But as you can see, there are several effective methods to restore your calendar. The easiest way is to restore from Google Calendar trash if the events were recently deleted. For older events, use data recovery software or sync calendar from your Gmail account. Following these simple steps, you can get back missing calendar events and appointments on your Android device.
