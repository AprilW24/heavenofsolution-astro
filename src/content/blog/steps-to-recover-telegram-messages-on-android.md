Telegram has become one of the most popular messaging apps for Android users. With its focus on privacy and security, along with useful features like groups, channels, and bots, it’s easy to see why so many people rely on Telegram for daily communication.

When you delete Telegram messages or conversations on your Android phone, they are not immediately erased from your device storage or Telegram’s servers. For a short period, it is possible to restore deleted Telegram data.

In this guide, I will outline the top techniques to recover deleted Telegram messages, including:

- Using Telegram’s built-in undo delete feature

- Checking the Telegram cache folder

- Restoring app data from a previous Android backup

- Using third-party Android data recovery apps

### 1. Using Telegram’s Undo Delete Feature

Telegram has a handy **Undo** feature that lets you quickly reverse a message deletion within 5 seconds. Here’s how it works:

- Delete a message in Telegram as usual by long pressing on it.

- You will see an **Undo** prompt appear at the bottom – tap it.

- The deleted message will be immediately restored in the chat.

While the 5 second window is very short, this is by far the fastest way to retrieve deleted Telegram messages on Android. It’s perfect for quickly fixing accidental deletions.

### 2. Checking the Telegram Cache Folder

Android stores cached Telegram data in an app-specific folder that you can access. There’s a chance recently deleted messages may still be cached.

To check the Telegram cache for deleted messages:

- Launch your Android file manager app.

- Navigate to Internal Storage/Android/data/org.telegram.messenger/cache.

- Browse and search for deleted Telegram files.

- Copy any recoverable Telegram files to another folder.

This Telegram cache check works if the messages were very recently deleted.

### 3. Restoring Deleted Messages from a Backup

If you have Telegram messages backed up on your Google Drive or SD card from another Android backup app, you may be able to restore deleted conversations from the backup.

The steps will vary depending on your backup tool. But in most cases you can:

- Open the backup tool and browse the available backups.

- Choose a backup that contains Telegram data.

- Tap restore – this will overwrite your device data with the backup.

- Your deleted Telegram messages should now be recovered.
