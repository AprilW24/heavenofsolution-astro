Trying to grab your Facebook profile link on Android but can’t find where it’s hiding? You’re not alone. After Facebook’s newer app updates, a lot of menu items have moved around, making it tricky to figure out where the copy link option lives.

This quick guide breaks down what to tap, where to look, and how to copy your Facebook account URL straight from your phone. Whether you want to share your profile with friends or drop your link in your bio somewhere else, these steps will get it done fast.

Here’s how you can grab that Facebook link from the Android app without getting lost in the menus. 📱

## Step-by-step to get your Facebook account URL on Android

- Open the Facebook app on your Android phone.

- Tap your profile icon in the top of screen to go to your profile.

- Once you’re on your profile, tap the three dots next to the Edit Profile button.

- Scroll down until you see the “**Your Profile Link**” option.

- Tap “**Copy Link**” to copy your Facebook account URL.

- You’ll see a quick message saying it’s copied to your clipboard.

- Now, paste it anywhere you want like Messenger, Notes, or your *Instagram* bio.

- If you want someone else’s link, go to their profile, tap the three dots, and tap “**Copy Link**” the same way.

- Done! You’ve got your Facebook account URL ready to share. 🎉

## What your Facebook URL looks like

Your Facebook account URL is what points directly to your profile. It’s usually something like https://www.facebook.com/your.username. If you changed your username before, that part after the slash will be your custom one. Otherwise, Facebook adds a string of numbers unique to your account.

You can always edit your username by going to Settings and clicking on Personal Information, then “Username.” Once you update it, your profile URL will change too. That’s why it’s good to double-check before sharing your link, especially if you want to keep it clean and easy to remember.

## How to share your account link easily

Once you’ve copied your Facebook link, you can send it through pretty much any app. You can drop it in a text, DM it on *WhatsApp*, or paste it into another social profile. If you run a business page, you can also put it on your website or email signature.

Sharing your Facebook link makes it simple for people to view your profile or page directly. No need to explain how to find you, just send the link. The same process works if you want to get someone else’s link too. Just open their profile, hit the three dots, and copy.

## Conclusion

Getting your Facebook URL on Android isn’t complicated once you know where to look. Just a few taps, and your link is ready to go wherever you want it.

Next time you update your bio or want to share your page, you’ll know exactly how to grab that link like a pro. 👍

## FAQs

### How do I find someone else’s Facebook link on Android?

Open their profile, tap the three dots next to the message button, then choose Copy Link.

### Can I change my Facebook profile URL?

Yes, you can edit it from Settings by selecting Username under Personal Information.

### Why can’t I see the Copy Link option?

Make sure you’ve updated the Facebook app. Older versions might not show that option.

### Does this work for business pages too?

Yes, you can copy the link from the business page the same way tap three dots, then Copy Link.

### How do I share my Facebook link to *Instagram*?

Go to your *Instagram* profile, tap Edit Profile, and paste your Facebook link into the website field.
