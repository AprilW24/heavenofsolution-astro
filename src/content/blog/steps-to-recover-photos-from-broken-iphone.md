Losing photos stored on your iPhone due to a broken screen or other damage can be extremely upsetting. Those photos may include irreplaceable memories and moments that you cherish. The good news is that there are methods to recover photos from a broken iPhone even without an iCloud backup.

### 1. Use iTunes Backup

The easiest way is to restore photos from your most recent iTunes backup if you have your iPhone connected to a computer. Here are the steps:

1. Connect your broken iPhone to your computer using a USB cable. iTunes should detect the device.

2. In iTunes, click on your iPhone icon and go to the Summary tab.

3. Click **Restore Backup** and select the latest backup file that contains your photos.

4. iTunes will restore data from that backup, including photos, to your iPhone or a new iPhone if replacing the broken one.

This allows you to recover photos without needing iCloud as long as you have a recent iTunes backup.

### 2. Use iPhone Data Recovery Software

If you don’t have an iTunes backup, third-party iPhone data recovery software offers another solution to retrieve photos. Here are the main steps:

1. Download and install data recovery software like iSkysoft or EaseUS MobiSaver on your computer.

2. Connect your broken iPhone to the computer using a USB cable.

3. Launch the data recovery software and scan your iPhone for recoverable data.

4. Preview and select the photos you wish to recover.

5. Save the retrieved photos to your computer or external storage.

The software scans the iPhone’s storage for recoverable data even if the screen is damaged. This is an effective way to restore photos from a broken device without an iTunes or iCloud backup.

### 3. Take Device to Repair Shop

As a last resort, you can take your broken iPhone to a phone repair shop. They have the skills and technology to potentially:

- Repair the screen and regain access to internal storage

- Extract data directly from the iPhone’s memory chips

- Transplant memory chips to a working iPhone

While costly, specialist mobile repair firms do offer iPhone data recovery solutions and may be able to rescue photos from severely damaged devices when all else fails.

### Conclusion

Losing precious photos due to iPhone damage can be devastating. But there are solutions for recovering photos from broken devices even without an iCloud backup. Using iTunes restore or data recovery software are two options that usually yield success. For extreme cases, mobile repair experts may be able to recover data from badly damaged iPhones. Be sure to regularly back up iPhone photos in the future to avoid this situation again. Following these tips, you have an excellent chance of rescuing important memories from your broken iPhone.
