Like putting emojis on Instagram chat, you also can put emojis on Instagram stories. When you make a story on Instagram, you can add an emoji to complete your sentence, picture, or video.

Putting emojis on Instagram stories is so easy. You can do it like these steps.

1. First, open your Instagram app and swipe right to access the camera mode.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video.jpg)

2. Next, take a photo or video by tapping one time on the white circle to take a picture and a long tap on the circle to take a video.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-2.jpg)

3. Other than that, You also can tap the gallery in the bottom left corner and select the picture or video you want to upload.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-3.jpg)

4. Finish taking or select a video or photo, you can click the face symbol on the top right corner.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-4.jpg)

5. Then, select the emoji icon.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-5.jpg)

6. Next, you can tap plus symbol on the bottom right corner to get more emojis.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-6.jpg)

7. After the emojis show, you can select the emoji you want to input by tapping the emoji or you can search the emoji by tapping the search column.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-7.jpg)

8. After selecting the emoji, you can tap **Done**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-8.jpg)

9. Then, set your emoji position by swiping it to where you want.

10. Next, click the arrow on the right bottom corner.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-9.jpg)

11. Last, tap **Share** to post your picture or video on your Instagram Stories.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/04/11-Steps-to-Put-Emoji-in-Instagram-Story-Picture-and-Video-10.jpg)

Now, your Instagram story has an emoji. You also can follow these steps to add more emojis or other stickers to your story.
