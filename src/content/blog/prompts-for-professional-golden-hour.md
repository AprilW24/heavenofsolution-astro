Golden hour is that short window right after sunrise or just before sunset, when the sun sits low and wraps everything in warm amber light. 🌅 If you’ve ever wanted pro-level golden hour portraits without booking a shoot, these prompts make it possible. I put these together for all types of photos, from solo portraits of a girl or a man, to couples, families, fashion editorials, and silhouettes.

To use any of these in Gemini, head to gemini.google.com, open a new chat, and paste the prompt straight in. 💬 Gemini generates the photo directly from the text, so the more detail in the prompt, the better the result. You don’t need a camera, a model, or a location. Just drop the prompt and let Gemini do the work. 📸

### 1. Professional Golden Hour Girl Portrait

*“A photorealistic portrait photograph of a young woman standing in an open wheat field during golden hour, 30 minutes before sunset. She wears a flowy cream sundress. Warm golden sunlight comes from directly behind her, creating a glowing rim light around her hair and silhouette. Soft natural lens flare visible in the frame. Her hair lifts gently in a light breeze. Background is a blurred golden wheat field with smooth bokeh. Shot on an 85mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field. Warm cinematic color grading with amber, gold, and orange tones. High resolution, professional portrait photography. 3:2 aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Professional-Golden-Hour.webp)

### 2. Golden Hour Side Lighting Male Portrait on a City Sidewalk

*“A photorealistic portrait photograph of a confident young man standing on a city sidewalk during golden hour. He wears a casual linen shirt with sleeves slightly rolled up. Warm golden side light hits him from the left, casting long shadows across the pavement and creating depth and contrast on his face. His expression is calm and relaxed. Background shows blurred city buildings bathed in warm amber light. Shot on an 85mm f/1.8 lens, shallow depth of field, natural bokeh. Warm cinematic color grading with deep amber and golden tones. High resolution, professional portrait photography. 3:2 aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Golden-Hour-Side.webp)

### 3. Romantic Couple Golden Hour Backlighting Portrait

*“A photorealistic couple portrait photograph taken on a sandy beach during golden hour, 20 minutes before sunset. A man and a woman stand close together, the man gently placing his hand on the woman’s waist. They look at each other and smile softly. Warm golden sunlight comes from directly behind them, creating a glowing rim light around both of their bodies. Soft lens flare visible. Ocean waves blur gently in the background. Shot on a 50mm f/1.4 lens, shallow depth of field, soft natural bokeh. Warm cinematic color grading with golden, amber, and soft rose tones. High resolution, professional couple portrait photography. 3:2 aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Romantic-Couple-Golden.webp)

### 4. Golden Hour Family Portrait in an Open Meadow at Sunset

*“A photorealistic family portrait photograph taken in an open green meadow during golden hour. A family of four, two parents and two young children, laugh and play together. Warm golden backlight wraps around all of them, creating soft glowing edges on their hair and shoulders. Long soft shadows stretch across the grass. Shot from a medium distance at a slightly low angle, 50mm f/2.0 lens, natural bokeh. Background shows golden trees out of focus. Warm natural color grading with soft amber and green tones. High resolution, professional family portrait photography. 3:2 aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Golden-Hour-Family.webp)

### 5. Golden Hour Fashion Editorial Portrait of a Woman

*“A photorealistic fashion editorial photograph of a tall woman standing on a hilltop during golden hour. She wears a long, flowing maxi dress in deep burgundy. The dress fabric moves in the wind. The sky behind her glows in shades of deep orange, amber, and soft gold. Her pose is strong and editorial, facing slightly away from the camera with her chin tilted up. Direct golden backlight creates a full rim light around her figure. Shot on a 70-200mm f/2.8 lens, compressed background, soft bokeh. High contrast warm cinematic color grading. High resolution, professional fashion editorial photography. 3:2 aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Golden-Hour-Fashion.webp)

### 6. Golden Hour Street Portrait of a Woman in a European Town

*“A photorealistic street portrait photograph of a young woman walking on a narrow cobblestone street during golden hour in a European old town. She wears a stylish beige trench coat and holds a small coffee cup. Warm golden light streams between old stone buildings, casting long shadows across the cobblestones and golden rays across the street. Background shows old European architecture bathed in warm amber light, out of focus. Shot on a 35mm f/1.8 lens, natural bokeh, candid feel. Warm cinematic color grading with amber and gold tones. High resolution, professional street photography. 3:2 aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Golden-Hour-Street.webp)

### 7. Couple Silhouette at Golden Hour Sunset on a Beach Pier

*“A photorealistic silhouette photograph of a couple standing together on a wooden beach pier during golden hour. Both silhouettes are sharp and clear against a blazing orange and gold sky. The sun sits just above the horizon, reflecting a long golden strip across the calm ocean water below. The man and woman hold hands, both facing the sunset. Dramatic sky with deep amber, orange, and pale yellow gradient from top to bottom. Shot on a 24mm wide angle lens, f/8 aperture, everything in sharp focus. High resolution, professional silhouette photography. 16:9 cinematic aspect ratio.”*

![](/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/HoS_Couple-Silhouette-at-Golden.webp)

Use this one when you want a dramatic, clean, and cinematic result with no face details needed.

These 7 prompts give you a solid starting point for generating pro-level golden hour photos on Gemini, no shoot required. 🌇 Feel free to swap the outfit, location, or lighting angle in any prompt to get exactly what you’re after.
