I’ve been testing Gemini AI for portrait creation lately, and the cozy indoor style gives some of the best results I’ve seen 😍. These prompts are built to generate warm, realistic indoor portraits from scratch, no reference photo needed, just text and creativity. You can use them for a woman, man, couple, or group shot depending on the vibe you’re going for.

To get started, go to **gemini.google.com** and open a new chat 💬. Paste any prompt below into the text box and let Gemini build the full scene for you. The clearer your details, the better the output will be, and these prompts are already written to get you strong results on the first try 🎯.
