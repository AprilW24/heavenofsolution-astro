If you’re working with videos in Canva and need to adjust the aspect ratio, especially on the mobile app, it can be a bit tricky since the mobile app doesn’t offer the same level of customization as the desktop version. However, you can still achieve your desired video ratio by following some simple steps. Let’s dive into how you can do it.

## Understanding Aspect Ratios

Before we begin, it’s important to understand what aspect ratios are and why they matter. Aspect ratios determine the shape of your video or image, with common ratios including 16:9 for horizontal videos and 1:1 for square videos. Different platforms require different aspect ratios for optimal display.

## Steps to Change Video Ratio in Canva Mobile App

Since the Canva mobile app doesn’t directly support changing aspect ratios like the desktop version, you’ll need to use a workaround. Here’s how you can do it:

1. **Open Canva on Your Desktop**: If possible, use the desktop version of Canva to change the aspect ratio. This is because the desktop version offers more features, including the ability to resize and change aspect ratios directly.

2. **Use the Desktop Version for Aspect Ratio Changes**:

- Open your project in Canva on your desktop.

- Click on the **Resize** button at the top.

- Enter the new dimensions for your desired aspect ratio (e.g., 1920×1080 for 16:9).

- Choose whether to create a new design or replace the current one.

3. **Workaround for Mobile App**:

- If you only have access to the mobile app, you can create a new design with the desired aspect ratio.

- Start by creating a new project with the custom dimensions you want.

- Copy elements from your original design and paste them into the new project.

4. **Use Presets for Common Aspect Ratios**:

- Canva offers presets for common platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook.

- Use these presets to ensure your video fits the platform’s recommended aspect ratio.

## Tips for Mobile Video Editing

- **Vertical Videos**: For mobile platforms like Instagram Reels or YouTube Shorts, consider using a 9:16 aspect ratio (1080×1920 pixels).

- **Square Videos**: For Instagram feed posts, use a 1:1 aspect ratio (1080×1080 pixels).

## Conclusion

Changing the aspect ratio in Canva’s mobile app requires a bit of creativity, but by using the desktop version or creating a new project with the desired dimensions, you can achieve the look you want. Remember to use presets for common platforms to ensure your videos display correctly.

Additionally, if you frequently work with videos on your mobile device, it might be worth exploring other video editing apps that offer more flexibility with aspect ratios. Apps like Adobe Premiere Rush or InShot provide robust features for mobile video editing, including easy aspect ratio adjustments. This can save you time and effort in the long run, especially if you’re working on projects that require precise control over video dimensions 📹.

## FAQs

### How Do I Change the Aspect Ratio in Canva on Desktop?

To change the aspect ratio in Canva on desktop, click the **Resize** button at the top, enter your new dimensions, and choose whether to create a new design or replace the current one.

### Can I Change the Aspect Ratio Directly in the Canva Mobile App?

No, the Canva mobile app does not support direct aspect ratio changes like the desktop version. You need to create a new project with the desired dimensions.

### What Are Common Aspect Ratios for Social Media?

Common aspect ratios include 16:9 for horizontal videos, 1:1 for square videos, and 9:16 for vertical videos.

### How Do I Use Presets in Canva?

To use presets, click on **Create a Design** and select from the available templates for platforms like YouTube, Instagram, or Facebook.

### Is Changing Aspect Ratio Available in Canva Free?

In the free version of Canva, you can’t directly change the aspect ratio using the resize feature. Instead, copy your design elements into a new project with the desired aspect ratio.
