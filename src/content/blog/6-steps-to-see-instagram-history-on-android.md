Instagram is a popular social media platform that allows users to share photos, videos, and stories with their followers. One feature that users may want to access is their account history, which includes information about their activity on the platform. In this article, we will discuss the steps to view your Instagram history on an Android device. Follow these steps to do so.

1. Launch the Instagram app on your Android device and log in to your account.

2. Tap your profile picture in the bottom right corner of the screen.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Instagram-Steps-HoS.jpg)

3. Tap the three horizontal lines in the top right corner.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Instagram-Steps-HoS-2.jpg)

4. Tap **Your activity**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Instagram-Steps-HoS-3.jpg)

5. Tap **Account history**.

![](/wp-content/uploads/2023/11/Instagram-Steps-HoS-4.jpg)

6. Scroll through the list of activities to view your account history, including changes to your bio, profile picture, and privacy settings.

By following these steps, you can easily access and view your Instagram account history on an Android device. This information can be useful for tracking your activity on the platform and understanding how your account has evolved over time. Remember to regularly review your account history to ensure your account remains secure and up-to-date.
